## 📥 How to Update

### Automatic Update (Recommended)
1. Open Collabrix v2.0.3
2. App will detect new version
3. Click "Download Update"
4. Click "Restart and Install"
5. Done! You're on v2.0.4

### Manual Update
1. Download `Collabrix-Setup-2.0.4.exe`
2. Run the installer
3. It will update your existing installation
4. Launch Collabrix
